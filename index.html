SnackSwap Thesis
ArduinoC++ESP32IoTBlynk
Reverse vending machine that accepts plastic bottles and aluminum cans in exchange for snacks or water — promoting recycling and community well-being.
  • Capacitive, inductive, IR, and weight sensors for 100% material classification accuracy
  • Arduino Mega processes validation; ESP32 streams bin & water levels to Blynk app in real time
  • Point-based reward system: 10 pts = snack, 3 pts = water; 2-min reset prevents point abuse
  • Ultrasonic sensors track fill levels with 95–99% accuracy, avg. 11.77 s response
  • 10 test users: usability 4.3/5, reward satisfaction 4.5/5, environmentally beneficial 5.0/5
  • Built with Sintra board prototype housing; total BOM cost ₱12,750
